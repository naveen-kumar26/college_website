PEO and PSO

Programme Educational Objectives

  • Possess a strong foundation in the mathematical, scientific and engineering fundamentals necessary to formulate, design, solve and analyze engineering problems.
  • Have exposure to emerging cutting edge technologies, adequate training and opportunities to work as teams on multidisciplinary projects with effective communication skills and leadership qualities.
  • Develop skills for a successful career and work with values & social concerns bridging the digital divide and meeting the requirements of the Corporate Industries.
  • Inculcate professional ethics and follow codes of professional practice and encourage life-long learning.

Program Specific Outcomes

  • Graduates will be able to design, develop, test and maintain software systems with the latest tools in the fields of IT.
  • Graduates will be able to apply the knowledge of academic learning to solve real life Engineering problems and find solutions for contemporary issues faced by society at large.
